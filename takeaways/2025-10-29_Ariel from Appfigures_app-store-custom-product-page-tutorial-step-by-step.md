## Key Takeaways
- Custom product pages (CPPs) deliver double-digit improvement in conversion rates, reducing cost of acquisition for paid campaigns
- 83% of top apps are using custom product pages, making it essential for competition
- You can create up to 35 custom product pages per app to target different user segments and use cases
- CPPs work by showing specific screenshots matched to search intent (e.g., basketball tickets â†’ Knicks-specific screenshots)
- Focus on showcasing different features/use cases rather than just changing colors - cater screenshots to the specific search query
- Start with a "poor man's approach" by shuffling existing screenshots to highlight relevant features first
- Target longtail keywords with CPPs while keeping main keywords on your default product page
- Use Apple Ads to quickly test which keyword + CPP combinations work best, then move successful ones to organic

## Action Items
- [ ] Analyze 2-3 top competitors in your category to see their custom product page strategies and targeted keywords
- [ ] Create your first CPP by shuffling existing screenshots to highlight your secondary use case or feature
- [ ] Set up custom product pages for 3-5 longtail keywords from your keyword list that represent different user segments
- [ ] Run Apple Ads campaigns with different CPP + keyword combinations to identify highest-converting pages before applying to organic
- [ ] Review your app reviews and demographics to identify distinct user groups that warrant separate custom product pages
